About Elektra

Elektra is a fully composable NFT cross chain bridging protocol which enables the seamless transfer of value across different blockchains.

We aim to create an NFT primitive that removes the tribal chain dependancy currently seen in the market and work towards building an omnichain future.

Use Cases

Elektra will enable users to take advantage of liquidity wherever it is and enable the transition of NFTs to be used in DeFi based applications such as lending markets and DEXs.

We believe the biggest use cases of our bridge is bringing interoperability in the following categories: gaming, lending, cross chain trading and general transfer of value.

DAO

Elektra aims to become a DAO governed by the community, EKA will play a central role in voting, incentivising early users and bootstrapping the network.

Stakers of EKA receive 100% of the protocol revenue. EKA liquidity providers will be able to lock their LP tokens to earn rewards.
